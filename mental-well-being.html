Mental Well-Being

Through a series of design research methods, our team identified social relationships and positivity are key elements in maintaining mental-well being. We designed a mobile app to focus on social interactions that evoke positive attitudes. The positive attitudes generated from these interactions are meant to help individuals feel more empowered as they proceed with stressful activities in their daily lives.

Design Research Methods

User Interviews

Conduced with the purpose of understanding users' relationships with mental well-being and their efforts to maintain a healthy state of mental well-being. User interviews consisted of 1:1 interviews with young adults who have recently experienced high levels of stress

“Mental well-being is achieved through a positive attitude”

Maintaining an appropriate balance of work, social relationships and sleep is key to maintaining mental well-being.

Co-design

Facilitated to help create a product that fits seamlessly into their lives. We worked collaboratively with a group of participants we considered as our “experts”. Our experts were cpomprised of individuals who were actively trying to achieve mental well-being on a daily basis.

Modified User Enactments

Inspired by User enactment methods (citation here). Employed a modified version to help further understand emotional reactions to receiving positive messages from close friends and gauge willingness to send similar messages. Recruited participants expressing high levels of stress. Mobile devices provided to act as low – fid prototypes. Team sat in separate room to orchestrate a series of text messages to these devices. Text messages designed to appear as if they are being sent by participants’ friends. Setting: participants in lab studying for quickly approaching finals.

Literature Review

Further support for social relationships as a means of creating mental well-being from existing research. “Relatedness”, which is important to well-being, can be generated through shared activities that reinforce relationships by creating shared experiences. A study of college dormitories showed a statistically significant positive correlation between interaction with others and feelings of purpose in life. Individuals who provide support to friends and see that their efforts were successful and appreciated experience increased positive emotions and meaning in life

Design Solution

The Mobile App

Our final design solution is a mobile app that fosters social relationships by sending and receiving positive messages to and from close friends. Our app utilizes an integrated analysis of users’ data to curate appropriate notifications. It is composed of four tabs: Home, Friends, Calendar and Invites.
