About

Cynthia is a UX Designer currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Information Science at Cornell University.

She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Anthropology from UC Irvine and a Certificate in Visual Media Design from the City College of San Francisco. Cynthia's background in Social Sciences provides a Human Centered foundation for her technical and design strategies.

She has over 5 years of industry experience working directly with designers, engineers and business stakeholders as a Digital Marketer.

In her free time, Cynthia enjoys making jewelry and eating tacos.
